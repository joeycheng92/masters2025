AUGUSTA NATIONAL GOLF CLUB
AUGUSTA, GEORGIA

To the Esteemed Participants of the 2025 Fantasy Masters,

As the final echoes of patronage appreciation fade across the Azaleas and the shadows lengthen over Rae's Creek, we conclude another Masters Tournament, marked by the enduring drama and spirit of competition that define this cherished week.

Championship Sunday unfolded with the characteristic intensity that only Augusta National on the final day can provide. It was a day of profound shifts in fortune, where the pressure inherent in pursuing a Green Jacket saw several leaders falter, creating opportunities for remarkable advances. None was more striking than that of Mr. Justin Rose, who navigated the course with exceptional skill, posting a six-under-par sixty-six to surge prominently onto the final leaderboard.

The ultimate triumph, however, belonged to Mr. Rory McIlroy. In a narrative arc defined by resilience, he confronted not only the formidable challenge of the course and his competitors but also the considerable weight of past experiences and childhood aspirations. His victory, secured resolutely on the first hole of a sudden-death playoff against Mr. Rose, represents a crowning achievement and the completion of the career Grand Slam.

The final round proved less favorable for others who began the day in contention. Mr. Bryson DeChambeau encountered difficulties, carding a three-over-par seventy-five that saw him slip down the final standings. We also observed the unfortunate conclusion to Mr. Ludvig Åberg's round; positioned near the lead, late scores on the seventeenth and eighteenth holes altered his final standing significantly.

These dramatic events on the course directly influenced the outcome of our Fantasy Masters. We extend our warmest congratulations to Mr. Bill Jin, this year's Fantasy Masters Champion. His strategic selections, particularly his faith in the eventual winner, Mr. McIlroy, propelled him to the top of the leaderboard. We also commend Mr. Ben Wedd, whose similar reliance on Mr. McIlroy's performance secured him a commendable second-place finish.

While Mr. Jin celebrates this particular triumph, we understand from observations within the league's own spirited discourse that his competitive endeavors extend beyond our fantasy competition, presenting varied challenges in other arenas – indeed, reports suggest a dynamic competitive environment exists even within his own household regarding rankings in other leagues, such as Formula 1.

We acknowledge last year's esteemed winner, Mr. Joey Cheng. As anticipated by some following the opening rounds, his significant investment in players such as Mr. DeChambeau and Mr. Scheffler led to a challenging final day and a tumble from the leading positions. The demanding nature of this year's competition, especially the repercussions of the cut after Friday's play, was evident across the final standings, with many participants concluding the tournament with positive scores due to reduced active player rosters. Amidst this, we note the respectable plus-one score achieved by Mr. Colin Roche, a solid midfield finish secured under rather unique circumstances that will likely provide fodder for future league recollections.

On behalf of the Committee, we wish to extend our sincere gratitude to all participants for their engagement and effort throughout this Fantasy Masters competition. Your participation brings this event to life. Furthermore, we would like to take this moment to extend special congratulations to Mr. Jack Traveerungroj on the joyous occasion of his recent engagement. We wish him and his fiancée all the very best for their future together.

The 2025 Masters Tournament is now etched into the annals of golf history. It has been our distinct privilege to host this engaging fantasy competition alongside the main event. In accordance with tradition, Mr. Joey Cheng respectably relinquishes the title, passing the honor and accompanying bragging rights to our 2025 Champion, Mr. Bill Jin.

We thank each of you for your participation and sportsmanship throughout the week and anticipate the return of this tradition next April.

Respectfully,

The Augusta National Golf Club
(On behalf of the Fantasy Masters Commissioner)
